## Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end data analytics workflow that explores customer shopping behavior to uncover purchasing trends, revenue drivers, and customer segments. The analysis combines Python, PostgreSQL, SQL, and Tableau to transform raw retail transaction data into an interactive business intelligence dashboard.

The objective is to help business stakeholders better understand customer behavior and support data-driven decisions related to marketing strategies, customer engagement, and product performance.

---

## Business Problem

A retail company wants to better understand its customers' shopping behavior in order to:

- Identify high-performing product categories
- Understand purchasing behavior across customer demographics
- Evaluate customer subscription trends
- Analyze seasonal purchasing patterns
- Support strategic business and marketing decisions

## Key Insights

- Product category is a major contributor to overall revenue.
- Customer purchasing behavior varies across different age groups.
- Seasonal shopping trends influence revenue performance.
- Subscription status provides valuable insight into customer purchasing habits.
- Payment methods show measurable differences in customer spending behavior.
